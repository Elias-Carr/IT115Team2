Benefits:

Valve is quite of a secretive company and doesn't list their official benefits on their website. However they are known to be one of the best game design companies to work at that offer a very flexible work schedule and the ability to work on whatever project the employee desires. Because of this, they keep their number of employees low at about 360 at the time of writing. This is incredibly small compared to other large game companies which have employee numbers in the thousands.
